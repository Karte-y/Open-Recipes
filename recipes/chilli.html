Chilli Recipe

One of my favorite foods

Here, I will describe the recipe for one of my favorite dishes to make. It's one of my favorites because the process is pretty simple as well as time efficient.

My Chilli recipe doesn't require many ingredients as you will see in the ingredients list below

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef or turkey (I prefer ground turkey breast)
  • 1 can of dark red kidney beans
  • 1 can of chilli ready diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 packet of any chilli seasoning of your choice. (I recommend simple truth)
  • 1/3 cup of olive oil
  1. Pour olive oil in sauce pan and bring it to medium heat
  2. After oil is slightly hot, dump your preffered choice of meat in the pan
  3. Allow the meat to cook for at least 15 minutes. After meat is done, add the can of diced tomatoes
  4. Add kidney beans, tomato sauce to saucepan
  5. Add seasoning packet, then stir till the contents are equally distributed
  6. Let simmer for about 20 minutes, but make sure to stir frequently yo prevent any sticky burning on the bottom
  7. Serve with anything you would want to go with your chilli, Enjoy!
