Honey Bourbon Steak tips

Another one of my favorite foods

Here I will show you how to make my famous honey bourbon steak tips.

This dish is one of my favorites due to its sweet and bouurbony flavor. especially when prepard with a quality cut of meat

Ingredients

  • 10oz black angus sirloin cut into sirloin
  • 1 vandalia onion bulb
  • 1/2 curp of honey
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper falkes
  • 3 table spoons of olive oil

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together honey, brown sugar, bourbon and red pepper flakes. Pour into a large ziptop bag. Add steak cubes and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat olive oil in pan over medium high heat
  3. Add steak and sliced onion to pan, cook until seared on all sides
  4. Remove from pan after it's done and serve with mashed potatoes or any side of choice
